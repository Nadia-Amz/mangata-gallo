Bracelets

It's all in the wrist. Our collection of bracelets features classic silhouettes and designs that push the boundaries of the imagination. Express your maximalist side with a strong stack that can't help but turn heads: a diamond tennis bracelet, chain link designs and polished bangles. If you want to make a powerful yet pared back statement, try wearing a wide cuff on each wrist. For a subtle approach, a few delicate styles in narrow widths always add up to something unexpected. When it comes to Tiffany bracelets, make your own style rules.

Necklaces

The long and the short of it is, Tiffany & Co. necklaces are the only finishing touch you need. Keep your style simple with striking essentials you'll wear everyday, like a diamond pendant or simple chain necklace that goes with everything. Add a long necklace or colorful gemstones the next time you want simple drama without trying. When you want to go all out, wear a mix of metals, lengths and styles for a look that's sure to strike up a conversation. From bold link chains to minimalist pendants, when it comes to Tiffany & Co. necklaces, one is never enough.

Earrings

Whether you want one statement or a whole earful, Tiffany & Co. earrings have a way of making people listen up. Each design is crafted with exceptional attention to detail, from classic Tiffany & Co. diamond studs to modern metal hoops. Go bold with a single pair of drop earrings that garner all the compliments, like sculptural pieces by Elsa Peretti or signature links from the Tiffany HardWear collection. Teach people a thing or two about geometry with a lineup of studs in different shapes and metals. And no one said matching was mandatory—two different styles brought together is an easy way to turn heads.
